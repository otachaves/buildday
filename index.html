FAQ

Quick answers.

Can I come alone? ▼
Absolutely. Most participants arrive solo. We create dedicated opportunities during the morning for you to meet other participants, join a team, and find a challenge that excites you. You won't be building alone.
Do I need to know how to code? ▼
No. The tools we use are designed for non-developers. Your AEC expertise — knowing the problem deeply — is worth more than coding skills. Technical participants are welcome too, but they're not required.
What if I don't have a team or idea? ▼
Most people arrive solo. Teams form in the morning based on which problems resonate. If you have an idea, people will rally around it. If you don't, you'll find a challenge that clicks.
Can I come with my own team? ▼
Absolutely. Arrive with a pre-formed group of up to 8 people and work on a shared challenge. Register individually and note your team at sign-up.
